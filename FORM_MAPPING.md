# TaxCopilot Form Mapping

This document explains how TaxCopilot maps to the product themes and job-style use cases it was designed to demonstrate.

## Product Positioning

TaxCopilot is:

- a local AI assistant for tax and bookkeeping teams
- an internal knowledge retrieval tool
- a bookkeeping review support system
- a client communication assistant
- a strategy content generation workflow

TaxCopilot is not positioned as:

- a generic chatbot
- a resume screener
- a fully automated accounting engine

## Module-To-Use-Case Mapping

### 1. Internal AI Brain

What it does:

- searches internal SOPs, client notes, strategy guides, and bookkeeping-related documents
- retrieves relevant chunks with local embeddings or keyword fallback
- answers questions with `Answer / Source / Proof`
- supports factual, yes or no, list, compound, assessment, and workflow questions

Job-theme alignment:

- internal AI tools
- document and SOP search
- knowledge retrieval for operations teams
- usable AI systems with evidence-backed output

### 2. Bookkeeping Copilot

What it does:

- reviews uploaded transaction CSVs
- detects missing values
- detects duplicate rows
- normalizes vendors and text fields
- suggests bookkeeping categories with rule-based logic
- flags rows needing human review

Job-theme alignment:

- bookkeeping efficiency
- workflow automation support
- practical AI-assisted triage
- human-in-the-loop accounting operations

### 3. Client Communication

What it does:

- generates structured client reports from selected internal documents
- produces summary, key issues, recommendations, action items, and a client email draft
- can also simplify the material with `Explain for Client`

Job-theme alignment:

- client communication
- usable AI systems
- operational support for client experience
- grounded drafting from internal material

### 4. Strategy Content Studio

What it does:

- turns internal strategy material into external-facing content
- supports email, explainer, newsletter, and social draft outputs
- keeps content grounded in the selected internal docs

Job-theme alignment:

- content and messaging support
- internal strategy reuse
- business-facing AI workflows

## Mapping To Common Form Themes

### Internal AI Tools

TaxCopilot demonstrates an internal AI tool that helps a team find process guidance, synthesize client notes, and support bookkeeping review without requiring cloud APIs.

### Document And SOP Search

The Internal AI Brain is purpose-built for internal document search and evidence-backed answers across SOPs, strategy notes, financial summaries, and client profiles.

### Workflow Automation

The product automates retrieval, summarization, issue surfacing, category suggestion, and draft creation while keeping human review in the loop.

### Bookkeeping Efficiency

The Bookkeeping Copilot reduces manual triage time by highlighting duplicates, missing categories, vendor normalization issues, and review rows in one place.

### Client Communication

The Client Communication module converts internal notes into concise, professional outputs that a tax or bookkeeping team can review before sending.

### Usable AI Systems

The system emphasizes:

- local execution
- clear module boundaries
- evidence-backed answers
- structured outputs
- human review instead of blind automation

## Why This Product Is Recruiter-Friendly

TaxCopilot shows:

- product framing, not just a technical demo
- domain-specific workflow thinking
- local AI integration with Ollama
- retrieval-augmented document use
- business-oriented UI and output structure

## Key Talking Point

`TaxCopilot is a local internal product concept for tax and bookkeeping teams. It combines document retrieval, bookkeeping review support, client communication drafting, and strategy reuse into one usable workflow assistant.`

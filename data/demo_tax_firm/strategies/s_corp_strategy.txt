S CORP STRATEGY GUIDE

When to Review:
Evaluate an S corporation strategy when business profit is consistently above $100,000 and owner compensation can be set at a reasonable level.

Potential Benefit:
An S corporation structure may reduce self-employment tax exposure on a portion of business income.

Requirements and Risks:
- Reasonable payroll must be run for the owner.
- State tax treatment should be reviewed before implementation.
- The added payroll and compliance work increases administrative complexity.
- The strategy should only be recommended after reviewing current profit stability and payroll capacity.

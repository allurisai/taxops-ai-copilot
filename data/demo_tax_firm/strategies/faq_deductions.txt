FAQ: COMMON DEDUCTION QUESTIONS

Q: Can meals be deducted?
A: Business meals may be deductible when they are ordinary, necessary, and properly documented.

Q: What should happen to mixed personal and business charges?
A: Split the transaction or move the unsupported portion to owner draw or shareholder distribution based on entity rules.

Q: How should recurring software expenses be handled?
A: Keep them categorized consistently each month and review for duplicate subscriptions.

CLIENT ONBOARDING SOP

Purpose:
Standardize the onboarding process for new tax and bookkeeping clients.

Step 1: Create the client record in the CRM and assign the service owner.
Step 2: Send the engagement letter, bookkeeping access request, and secure document checklist.
Step 3: Collect prior-year tax returns, entity documents, payroll reports, and bank or credit card access.
Step 4: Confirm accounting system access, including QuickBooks Online or the client bookkeeping portal.
Step 5: Hold a kickoff call to review reporting cadence, open bookkeeping issues, and current filing deadlines.
Step 6: Mark onboarding complete only after the source documents are received and the kickoff checklist is signed off.

Escalation:
If bookkeeping access is still missing after five business days, escalate to the client lead and send a reminder.

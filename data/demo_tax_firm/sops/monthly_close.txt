MONTHLY CLOSE SOP

Objective:
Complete a reliable monthly close for bookkeeping clients.

Step 1: Confirm all bank, credit card, and payment processor feeds have synced.
Step 2: Review uncategorized transactions and clear duplicate imports.
Step 3: Reconcile bank accounts and investigate any unmatched balance.
Step 4: Review payroll, owner draws, loan activity, and sales tax entries.
Step 5: Compare the current month profit and loss statement to the prior month for unusual swings.
Step 6: Prepare the close summary and note any follow-up items for the client.

Control note:
Do not finalize the close if material transactions are still uncategorized.

CLIENT REPORTING SOP

Purpose:
Prepare consistent monthly reporting for bookkeeping and tax advisory clients.

Steps:
1. Review the finalized profit and loss statement, balance sheet, and cash summary.
2. Identify bookkeeping issues that could affect tax planning, including uncategorized expenses, missing receipts, or unusual owner reimbursements.
3. Summarize the top three issues, the main recommendation, and any action items requiring client input.
4. Draft the client update in clear language and avoid unsupported tax advice.
5. Route the report to the client lead for review before sending.
